About NeoTech

NEO Tech

Neo-Tech club, a computer science society, an initiative of City Montessori School, Gomti Nagar-1, Lucknow. We aim to train students from junior to the senior section and help them develop skills which are not part of the academic curriculum of the school but will be beneficial to students in their future careers in the field of IT.

About CMS

About CMS

City Montessori School

City Montessori School (CMS) is a co-ed K-12 day school running in 18 campuses across the city of Lucknow, the state capital of Uttar Pradesh. Founded in 1959 by Drs Jagdish and Bharti Gandhi with the motto Jai Jagat.
The school is affiliated to the Delhi-based Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE) which offers the ICSE (class 10) and ISC (class 12) qualifications.
